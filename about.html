ABOUT KNOT PLANNERS

Planning and designing weddings that feel authentic and a true reflection of the client.We don't want your wedding event to feel overly produced,we want it to feel like an intimate dinner party with all of your closest family and friends.

KNOT is a full-service wedding event design and planning company founded in 2020. Our founder, ROSE HILROY, is a leader in the world of event planning and, as a sought after speaker, you will find her frequenting the main stage of the top industry conferences.

Understated elegance encompassed by effortless luxury is a knot signature. Through layering colors, textures, subtle patterns, and patina–from the printed suite to the tablescape–our events are known for the warm glow of candlelight coupled with a thoughtful, residential style. Every element, from the finish of the flooring to the hand of the linen draping, is carefully composed. We want our brides to look back at their weddings 15 years later and feel that their wedding was timeless.We work with the finest vendors in the business. Whether it be floral design, photography, entertainment or catering — our planning approach includes regular creative discussions with each vendor to ensure your wedding is one-of-a-kind.

What do we do?


We’re more than just a boutique event-planning firm. We’re a global team with the creative capacity to design and execute an unforgettable party — whether a destination wedding, a normal wedding or your big-bash party. We believe in creating unique moments that inspire.


What makes us different?


We listen to you. We then design and create distinctive events based on your specific needs and desires. We transform environments with our mix of solutions that integrate lighting, furnishings, linens and floral design. And we work with some of the best designers, fabricators and chefs in the business to bring experiences to life. We see our partners as more than just collaborators — they’re our friends. As are you.
